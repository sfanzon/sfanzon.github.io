Our preprint <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.14637">Time-rank duality in a simple model for Formula 1 racing</a> is out. We provide a novel approach  
to estimate race-winning probabilities, which leads to new insights regarding driver-level versus car-level effects, as well as a simplified Monte Carlo simulation algorithm. The annotated R code to reproduce the findings is <a href="https://sfanzon.github.io/F1-Paper-Code/">here</a>
